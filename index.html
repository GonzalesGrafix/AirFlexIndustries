About Us

A Legacy of Excellence
Since 1948

Air Flex Industries, located in Pomona, California was established in 1948. We began as a distributor of specialty aircraft electrical components and have since evolved into manufacturers of flexible electrical conduit assemblies, mil-spec conduit, related AN, LS and MS electrical fittings, backshell adapters and specialty fittings.

Our products are proudly made in the U.S.A., ensuring the highest quality standards and supporting American manufacturing.
